About Me

I am a PhD student in the School of Arts, Media and Engineering at Arizona State University. I am member of the Meteor Studio Research Lab, which explores the research and design of software and hardware for mobile Augmented Reality, Virtual Reality, and Mixed Reality. My research interests revolve around immersive data visualization for complex, layered datasets. I recently held a Visiting Student Researcher Position at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory where I designed and developed a Virtual Reality Mars image viewer to assist Rover Planning in the context of Mars mission operations. Outside of work, you can find me practicing mauy thai or teaching a yoga class.
